# Welcome to Tandem

Tandem is a self-hosted productivity app built around the **Getting Things Done (GTD)** methodology. It helps you capture, organize, and complete your work with clarity and confidence.

## What is GTD?

Getting Things Done is a personal productivity system created by David Allen. The core idea: get everything out of your head and into a trusted system, then process it into actionable next steps. Tandem implements the full GTD workflow:

1. **[[capture|Capture]]** — Throw anything into your Inbox without thinking about it
2. **[[clarify|Clarify]]** — Process inbox items into tasks, projects, or reference material
3. **[[organize|Organize]]** — Assign contexts, energy levels, and due dates
4. **[[reflect|Reflect]]** — Weekly reviews keep your system current and trustworthy
5. **[[engage|Engage]]** — Pick the right task for your current context and energy

New to GTD? Read the full [[what-is-gtd|What Is GTD?]] guide for a deeper introduction to the methodology and how Tandem implements each step.

## Your First Steps

### 1. Process Your Inbox

Head to the **Inbox** and add a few items — anything on your mind. Then process each one:
- Is it actionable? Create a task or add it to a project.
- Is it reference material? Add it to your personal Wiki.
- Is it something you're waiting on? Create a Waiting For item.

### 2. Set Up Contexts

Contexts are the GTD way of filtering tasks by where you are or what tools you have available. Go to **Contexts** to create ones that match your life:
- `@Computer` — tasks you do at your desk
- `@Phone` — calls and messages
- `@Errands` — things to do when you're out
- `@Home` — household tasks

### 3. Create Your First Project

Any outcome that requires more than one step is a GTD project. Go to **Projects** and create one. Add tasks to it, and Tandem will track the next action for you.

### 4. Do a Weekly Review

The Weekly Review is the heartbeat of GTD. Go to **Weekly Review** to walk through the guided process. This is how you keep your system trustworthy.

## Key Features

- **Inbox** — Quick capture for thoughts and tasks
- **Projects** — Multi-step outcomes with sequential or parallel task flows
- **Contexts** — Filter tasks by location or tool
- **Horizons of Focus** — Six levels of perspective, from daily actions to life purpose
- **Weekly Review** — Guided review process
- **Wiki** — Personal knowledge base with markdown and wikilinks
- **AI Integration** — Connect Claude via MCP for AI-assisted task management
